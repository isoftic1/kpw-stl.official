Get a Deep, Long-Lasting Clean with Soft Washing from Kosomax in St. Louis. Our Proven Method is Safe, Effective and Environmentally Friendly.

Residential & Commercial Soft Washing

Soft washing is a gentle yet effective method of cleaning exterior surfaces such as roofs, siding, and decks. It uses a low-pressure stream of water and specialized cleaning solutions to safely remove dirt, grime, and stains without causing damage. At Kosomax Power Wash, we offer soft washing services for both residential and commercial properties in the St. Louis area. By choosing soft washing, you can expect a deep and thorough cleaning that will leave your property looking like new, without the risk of damage that can come with high-pressure washing.

Benefits you can expect

  1. Enhance Curb Appeal: Pressure washing can remove dirt, grime, and stains from your property's exterior surfaces, giving it a fresh, new look and enhancing its curb appeal.
  2. Extended lifespan: Regularly soft washing can help protect your property from the damaging effects of dirt, grime, and other environmental factors, potentially extending its lifespan.
  3. Safer cleaning: With soft washing, there is less risk of damage to delicate surfaces, and it is a safer option for both the cleaning technicians and the property owners.
