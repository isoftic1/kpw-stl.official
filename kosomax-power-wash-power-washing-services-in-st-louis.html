Residential & Commercial Pressure Washing

Kosomax Power Wash offers professional pressure washing services for both residential and commercial properties in St. Louis. Pressure washing is a powerful method that uses high-pressure water to blast away dirt and grime from surfaces such as concrete, brick, and more. By removing the built-up dirt and stains, pressure washing can refresh the appearance of your property and improve its curb appeal. But that's not all - pressure washing can also protect your property from damage caused by mold, mildew, and other harmful substances. With Kosomax Power Wash's pressure washing services, you can enjoy a clean, healthy, and beautiful property that you can be proud of.

‍

Benefits you can expect

  1. Enhance Curb Appeal: Pressure washing can remove dirt, grime, and stains from your property's exterior surfaces, giving it a fresh, new look and enhancing its curb appeal.
  2. Protect Your Investment: Pressure washing can help prevent damage caused by harmful substances like mold, mildew, and algae, extending the life of your property's exterior surfaces and protecting your investment.
  3. Improved Health and Safety: Pressure washing can eliminate harmful substances that can cause allergies and health issues, making your property a safer and healthier place for your family or customers.
